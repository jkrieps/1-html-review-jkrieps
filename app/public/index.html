About John

John Doe was born in Port Moresby, Papau New Guinea on November 8th, 1981. Shortly after his birth, the Doe family moved to Bloomington, IN in order for his parents to advance their career as professors at Indiana University. Both of the parents were professionals in IT, having travelled all over the world giving seminars, so naturally they began teaching at the MSIS program . Because of this, John Doe grew up around technology his entire life. He began programming at the age of 12, and graduated at the top of his high school class. He then went on to pursue and undergrad degree at the Kelley School of Business in Information Systems, followed by an MSIS degree. Now, John is a partner at EY, consistently assisting various fortune 1000 companies with their technology implementations. He is also currently pursuing a PHD in Information Systems at Indiana University.
